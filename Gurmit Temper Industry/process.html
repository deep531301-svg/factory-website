Our Process

Heating → Quenching → Hardness Test → Cleaning → Oil Coating

Rotary Furnace Heating

Metal components are heated in a rotary furnace to achieve the required temperature for heat treatment.

Water Quenching

After heating, the material is quenched in water to increase hardness and improve strength.

Rockwell Hardness Testing

Material hardness is checked using a Rockwell hardness testing machine to ensure proper results.

Surface Cleaning

The material is cleaned to remove scale and surface impurities after heat treatment.

Protective Oil Coating

Protective oil is applied to prevent rust and maintain surface quality.
